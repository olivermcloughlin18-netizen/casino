♠ Blackjack

Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over.

Card Values

  • Number cards (2–10) = face value
  • Jack, Queen, King = 10 each
  • Ace = 1 or 11

How to Play

  1. Click chips to build your bet, then press Deal
  2. You and the dealer each get 2 cards. One dealer card is face-down
  3. Hit — take another card
  4. Stand — keep your current hand
  5. Double Down — double your bet, receive one more card
  6. If your total exceeds 21, you bust and lose
  7. Dealer must hit on 16 or below, stand on 17+

♠ Texas Hold'em

Setup

$200 buy-in. You play against 3 AI opponents. Blinds: Small $5, Big $10.

Betting Rounds

  1. Pre-Flop — 2 hole cards dealt.
  2. Flop — 3 community cards revealed.
  3. Turn — 1 more community card.
  4. River — Final community card.
  5. Showdown — Best 5-card hand wins the pot.

Actions

Check — Pass. Call — Match current bet. Raise — Increase bet. Fold — Give up hand.
